To analyze road accident data to identify patterns in casualties based on vehicle type, road surface, and location. Tools Used: [ Power BI] Data Scope: 196K Total Casualties across the UK, comparing 2021 and 2022. Key Findings: Significant reduction in fatal casualties (-35.6%) and a high concentration of incidents in urban environments during daylight hours.
